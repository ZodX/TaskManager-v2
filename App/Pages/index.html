Task Manager --v2

This is the TaskManager --v2 webapplication.

Here you can manage your tasks easily! You can set up workspaces and separate groups for your tasks in every one of them. Down below you can add your first tasks or list all the task(regardles of workspace and group) with the "Add your tasks!" button.

You get the list of the groups from a specific workspace, if you click on the "List Groups" button, on one the card of an already existing workplace. There you can list the tasks from one of the groups. On that page you get a full list of the tasks that you have in progress in the specified group.

You can add tasks to this group/workspace or to an other(obviously that won't show up here), after you filled all the fields correctly(Priority betveen 1-9). After that, you can update, delete or mark as complete every task you already have. You can change the ordering by clicking on the name ot the attribute('Task', 'Group', 'Priority', 'Last Modified').

On every page, the title brings you back here and there is an experimental "Darkmode" feature, what you can turn on with the black switch. Plus there is a 'Back' button which goes back one page in the hierarhy (e.g. table.html -> groups.html or groups.html -> index.html).
